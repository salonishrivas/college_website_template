Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2022

At the intersection of numbers and values, we challenge you to do more—to look beyond the bottom line and lead with principle and conviction. With the WashU Executive MBA, you’ll learn to rely on a values-based, data-driven decision-making framework. You’ll hone your individual leadership style though assessments, coursework and executive coaching. You’ll gain management expertise. And you’ll develop lasting professional relationships and collaboration.
Our EMBA program is designed for high-achieving, dedicated leaders. Classes meet once a month on Thursday, Friday and Saturday. In addition to class days, most students spend 10 to 15 hours each week completing assignments. Much of your out-of-class time is spent reading cases and preparing for class. And many student teams get together once a week. All the while, you’ll create a network of accomplished classmates, top-notch faculty, engaged WashU alumni and diverse business leaders.
